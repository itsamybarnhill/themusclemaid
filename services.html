01 — Recurring

Standard Clean

Refresh & Maintain

Our Standard Clean is designed to keep your home consistently fresh, clean, and comfortable. Perfect for recurring weekly, biweekly, or monthly maintenance visits.

Includes

  • Kitchen surface cleaning & sanitizing
  • Bathroom cleaning & sanitizing
  • Dusting of accessible surfaces
  • Vacuuming & mopping
  • Bed making & light tidying
  • Trash removal
  • Spot cleaning of mirrors, fixtures & high-touch areas

Ideal For

  • Routine home maintenance
  • Busy professionals & families
  • Homes cleaned regularly
02 — Detailed

Deep Clean

Detailed Reset Service

Our Deep Clean goes beyond routine maintenance and is designed to bring the home to a refreshed, detailed standard. Recommended for first-time clients, seasonal resets, or homes needing extra attention.

Includes Everything in Standard PLUS

  • Interior appliance cleaning
  • Detailed kitchen & bathroom cleaning
  • Baseboards, trim, and door detailing
  • Additional buildup removal
  • Detailed dusting throughout the home
  • Light fixture and blind detailing
  • Extra attention to high-use areas

Ideal For

  • First-time cleans
  • Spring cleaning
  • Homes needing a reset
  • Preparing for recurring maintenance service
